Share and Collaborate

You’ll never work alone.

As SEO Manager, you are a team player dealing with many departments within your organization or with third-party consultants. You also work on different projects, meaning that a perfect communication is key to your success.

We created the Team features just for you, and your (potentially extended) team. Sharing and reporting made easy.

With Botify, you can:

  • Share all sections of any reports simply by sending the appropriate URL link like: www.botify.com/user/website.com/report/. Easy.
  • Export any indicator or set of URLs of your analyses (eg: HTTP status code, URLs with duplicate HTML tags, URLs with only 1 follow incoming link, etc.).
  • Grant access to the application to team members with the appropriate authorization level for each project:
    • Guest: guest rights allow to view reports and use the URL Explorer.
    • Admin: admin rights allow to edit a project and start new analyses.
    • Owner: owner rights include Project editing, Team management and billing. There is only one owner per project.
