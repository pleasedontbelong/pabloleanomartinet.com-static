Discover areas of your website not crawled by Googlebot

Find out your crawl rate broken down by tag
The crawled pages ratio is the number of pages Googlebot sees versus the total number of pages on your site. The information is displayed by type of URLs in Botify so that you can identify the categories that urgently require optimization for a higher crawled pages ratio.

For example, if the category that accounts for 50% of your SEO traffic represents only 30% of the Googlebot crawl volume, then 70% of the pages with the most active category aren't even seen by Googlebot! Achieving a 100% crawl rate should become an immediate priority...

Relink or block Orphan Pages crawled by Googlebot

Find orphan pages that are crawled by Googlebot
Orphan pages are pages crawled by Googlebot that are not linked to from within your site. Googlebot can continue to crawl them if they appear in sitemaps, if there are links to them from other websites, a 200 code, etc.

Botify will show you that the SEO performance of these pages is weaker than that of the other pages in your site.
