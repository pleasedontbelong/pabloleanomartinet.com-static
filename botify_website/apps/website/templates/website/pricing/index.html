Frequently Asked Questions

How long does my subscription last?

Subscribing to Botify requires a one-month commitment. You can upgrade, downgrade or delete plans at any time.

Can I buy a year's subscription for a reduced rate?

Yes, and it's very easy. You get a 20% discount if you subscribe and pay for a whole year. You can pay by bank/credit card, wire transfer or paper check, whichever's easiest for you.

What payment methods and currencies do you accept?

We currently accept payment by bank/credit card and wire transfer (only for advance payments). We plan to accept PayPal by the end of the semester. You can pay in euros, US dollars and pounds sterling. If you have payment-related questions, don't hesitate to use our contact form.

How do I make a payment by wire transfer?

It's very easy and very fast. The sales team will send you a pro-forma invoice and our bank information. Your account is activated once the payment has been made. Please contact our sales team through our contact form.
